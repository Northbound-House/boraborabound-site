About

Hi, I'm Zac

Travel designer, advisor, and full-time adventurer — here to turn your travel dreams into reality.

My story

Strategy by trade, wanderer by heart

With a background in project management and strategy, I bring organization, attention to detail, and genuine care to every trip I design. I love the planning as much as the destination — and that means you get to skip the stress and enjoy the anticipation.

Cruises, tranquil beaches, and sun-soaked paradises are just some of my favorite escapes. My husband Chad and I live full-time in our RV, traveling across the U.S. and chasing new horizons — so I understand firsthand what it means to make travel a way of life.

My mission is simple: to transform your travel aspirations into memorable experiences through expertise, insider access, and a personal touch you won't find on a booking website.
